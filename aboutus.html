Our
Evolution

Born in Nigeria as a VAS aggregator, and
solidified its position as one of the top VAS
aggregators in the country. By H2, 2023 we had
become a super aggregator in Nigeria and
covering multiple services around mobile
technology. With the effective launch in 2024 of
ADtech, Bulk SMS, USSD API, and Airtime/Data
reselling, Pisi consolidated its position as a
single point of entry to multiple enterprise
services 2025 and Beyond: Payment processing
to complete the existing carrier billing services.
Aggregation of multiple financial services and
providing access to multiple mobile & digital
payment services to businesses and individuals.
